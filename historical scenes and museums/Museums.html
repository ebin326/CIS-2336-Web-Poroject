Historical Sites

Visiting historical sites in Houston offers a fascinating glimpse into the city's cultural heritage, as well as the broader history of Texas and the United States. It can be a fun and educational activity for people of all ages, with many sites offering interactive exhibits and engaging activities. Whether you're a history buff or simply curious about the past, it's an experience not to be missed.

Space Center Houston

Space Center Houston is a popular destination that played a crucial role in the development of space exploration and the Apollo missions. Visitors can tour the facility, see actual spacecraft and artifacts from the space program, and learn about the science and technology behind space travel.


San Jacinto Battleground

The San Jacinto Battleground State Historic Site is a significant historical site in Houston, commemorating the Battle of San Jacinto, the decisive battle of the Texas Revolution. Visitors can explore the battleground, see the San Jacinto Monument, and learn about the history of the conflict and its impact on Texas and the United States.

Heritage Society Museum

The Heritage Society Museum provides insights into the lives of the people who lived and worked in Houston in the past, and showcases the city's unique character and traditions. Visitors can tour restored historic homes, learn about the city's early settlers, and see period artifacts and furnishings.
