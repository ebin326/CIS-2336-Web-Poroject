Korea Garden


Korea Garden Restaurant is a popular Korean restaurant. The restaurant offers
a wide variety of Korean dishes, including traditional favorites like bibimbap,
bulgogi, and kimchi, as well as other popular Korean dishes like seafood pancakes,
spicy pork ribs, and grilled-it-yourself BBQ. The restaurant has a cozy and welcoming
atmosphere, and the staff is known for their friendly and attentive service.
Whether you are a fan of Korean food or looking to try something new, Korea Garden
Restaurant is definitely worth a visit.

Vic & Anthony's Steakhouse is a premier fine dining restaurant located in the heart of
downtown Houston, Texas. The restaurant features an upscale and modern atmosphere,
offering guests an elegant and comfortable dining experience. Known for its exquisite
steaks, Vic & Anthony's sources only the finest cuts of meat and serves them alongside
fresh seafood, salads, and sides. The extensive wine list includes selections from around
the world and complements the menu. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner
for two or a sophisticated setting for a business meeting, Vic & Anthony's Steakhouse is
the perfect choice.
