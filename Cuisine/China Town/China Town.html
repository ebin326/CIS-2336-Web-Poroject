Kim Son Restaurant


Kim Son is a popular Vietnamese restaurant in Houston, Texas
that has been serving delicious traditional Vietnamese cuisine
since 1982. With multiple locations throughout the city, Kim Son
is known for its extensive menu of authentic dishes, such as pho,
banh mi, and spring rolls, as well as its welcoming atmosphere and
attentive service. The restaurant also offers catering services and
private event spaces for special occasions.

Mein is a modern Chinese restaurant located in Houston, Texas, that offers
a unique fusion of traditional Chinese cuisine with contemporary twists.
The restaurant's menu features a wide variety of dishes, including dim sum,
noodles, rice bowls, and seafood, all made with fresh, high-quality ingredients.
The atmosphere at Mein is stylish and upscale, making it a popular spot for both
casual dining and special occasions. The restaurant also has a full bar with
a selection of cocktails, beer, and wine to complement the food.
