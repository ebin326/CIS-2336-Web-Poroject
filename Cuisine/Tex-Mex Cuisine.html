Tacos La Bala


Taco La Bala is a popular Mexican restauran that serves authentic
Mexican street food.The restaurant is known for its delicious and
affordable tacos, burritos, quesadillas, and other Mexican specialties.
Taco La Bala has a casual, laid-back atmosphere with indoor and
outdoor seating options. The restaurant has become a go-to spot
for locals and tourists alike who are looking for a taste of Mexico
in Houston. With its friendly service, delicious food, and affordable
prices, Taco La Bala has established itself as one of the best places
to grab a quick bite in Houston.
