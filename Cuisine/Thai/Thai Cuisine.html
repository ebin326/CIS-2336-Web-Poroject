Rim Tanon


Rim Tanon is a highly regarded Thai restaurant located in Houston, Texas.
The restaurant offers a modern take on traditional Thai cuisine, with an
emphasis on fresh ingredients and bold flavors. Some of their most popular
dishes include pad Thai, green curry, and tom yum soup. The atmosphere of
the restaurant is sophisticated yet inviting, with a cozy dining room and
friendly staff. Rim Tanon is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to
experience authentic and delicious Thai food in Houston.
