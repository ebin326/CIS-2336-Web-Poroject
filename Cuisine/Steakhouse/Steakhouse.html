Pappas Bros. Steakhouse


Pappas Bros. Steakhouse is a renowned fine dining restaurant. The restaurant
is famous for its exceptional selection of prime beef, dry-aged in-house and
grilled to perfection over an open flame. In addition to their delicious steaks,
Pappas Bros. Steakhouse also offers an extensive wine list featuring over 3,900
selections from around the world, as well as a variety of seafood, lamb, and other
upscale dishes. With its elegant decor and impeccable service, Pappas Bros. Steakhouse
has become a popular destination for special occasions, business dinners, and discerning
foodies in Houston.

Vic & Anthony's Steakhouse is a premier fine dining restaurant located in the heart of
downtown Houston, Texas. The restaurant features an upscale and modern atmosphere,
offering guests an elegant and comfortable dining experience. Known for its exquisite
steaks, Vic & Anthony's sources only the finest cuts of meat and serves them alongside
fresh seafood, salads, and sides. The extensive wine list includes selections from around
the world and complements the menu. Whether you're looking for a romantic dinner
for two or a sophisticated setting for a business meeting, Vic & Anthony's Steakhouse is
the perfect choice.
