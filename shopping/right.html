The Galleria

The Galleria is a shopping center with around 400 shopping outlets. It has been a booming area for Houstonians for the past 50 years. From the luxury outlets to the small stands selling handmade goods.

Thrifting

Thrifting is a very integral part of the young Houston culture.

Vintage Shopping

Lots of shops around Houston sell antique and vintage goods.

Cultural Marts

In many local areas in the city, there are many cultural shops that offer imports goods, and specialties from their country.
