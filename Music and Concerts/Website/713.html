About 713 Music Hall

Live music and entertainment return to POST with 713 Music Hall, a state-of-the-art, 5,000 person capacity concert venue. Located in the east wing of the USPS' sprawling former headquarters in Houston, the venue balances bold architecture with the raw industrial character of the historic facility.
