About Minute Maid Park

Minute Maid Park, nicknamed The Juice Box, is a retractable roof stadium in Houston, Texas, United States. It opened in 2000 as the home ballpark of Major League Baseball's Houston Astros, and hosts various music concerts of various artist. It has a seating capacity of 41,168, which includes 5,197 club seats and 63 luxury suites.
