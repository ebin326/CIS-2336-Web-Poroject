About AxelRad

Housed in the premises of a former grocery store built by the Axelrad family in the early 1920s. An expansive garden hosts a variety of video art, film screenings, live music, events, festivals, and more. It also features a nice selection of drinks and is located next to Luigi's Pizzeria
