About Toyota Center

Toyota Center is an indoor arena located in Houston. It is named after the Japanese automobile manufacturer Toyota. The arena is home to the Houston Rockets of the National Basketball Association (NBA). The arena can seat 18,104 for a basketball game, 17,800 for ice hockey games, and 19,300 for concerts.
