About Cynthia Woods Mitchell Pavilion

The Cynthia Woods Mitchell Pavilion caters to both the performing arts and contemporary artists and is also available for rental. It is owned and operated by The Center for the Performing Arts at The Woodlands and is a non-profit organization. The amphitheatre seats 16,500 people and has been the second-most heavily used amphitheater in the world.
