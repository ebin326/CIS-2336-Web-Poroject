George Observatory Stargazing
21901 FM 762 Rd, Damon, TX 77430
The observatory is open to the public on Saturday nights only, so making it a unique weekend experience in Houston. You can enjoy real-time camera feeds from the telescopes, expert discussions with astronomers, and a laser tour of the Milky Way and night sky, depending on the time of year.
Open Saturday 3pm-10pm
