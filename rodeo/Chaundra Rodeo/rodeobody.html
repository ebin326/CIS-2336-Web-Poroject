“Let’s Rodeo Houston!” is a saying we all know and love, the Rodeo is a big part of the Houston culture and has been for quite some time. Since 1932, the Houston Rodeo has become the largest stockshow and rodeo event not only in Texas but in the world. Now the Rodeo welcomes over 2 millions people each year and hosts more than 300 vendors.


The Houston Rodeo showcases a variety of entertainers and performers so there is something for everyone to enjoy. A few well known performers that have been welcomed to the rodeo in the past are Pitbull, Taylor Swift, George Strait and Florida Gerogia Line.

Visitors enjoy attending the Houston Rodeo due to its large size and capacity as well as the numerous events that take place during the rodeo. Visitors can visit the livestock and horse shows or grab a turkey at one of the many food stands available. There is also a well known carnival that takes places as well as concerts, shopping vendors and wildlife expos. There is tons the Houston Rodeo has to offer, you won’t want to miss it!
